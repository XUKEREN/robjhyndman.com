Coherent population forecasting using R

24 July 2014

demography, forecasting, R, statistics

This is an example of how to use the demography package in R for stochastic population forecasting with coherent components. It is based on the papers by Hyndman and Booth (IJF 2008) and Hyndman, Booth and Yasmeen (Demography 2013). I will use Australian data from 1950 to 2009 and forecast the next 50 years. In demography, “coherent” forecasts are where male and females (or other sub-groups) do not diverge over time.
