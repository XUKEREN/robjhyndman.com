What to do when the PhD is finished?

10 February 2010

So you’re wondering what to do once your PhD is finally completed? First, take a holiday. Completing a PhD is an intense and draining exercise, and you should take some time to refresh. Then you need to decide what career path you prefer. In statistics, there are three choices: the academic route, the semi-academic route and the business route.
